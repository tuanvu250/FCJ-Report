### Week 1 Objectives:

- **Onboarding & Setup**: Get familiar with FCJ culture, prepare the environment (VS Code, Git, CLI), and set up secure AWS accounts (IAM, MFA).
- **Networking Foundations**: Master VPC, Subnet, Route Table, NAT Gateway, and core networking components.
- **Compute Services**: Deploy EC2, establish SSH connections, and understand Global Infrastructure and VM types.
- **Hybrid DNS & Connectivity**: Set up Route 53 Resolver for Hybrid environments and configure Site-to-Site VPN labs.
- **Compute Deep Dive**: Dive deep into Auto Scaling, EFS, FSx, and other compute services.
