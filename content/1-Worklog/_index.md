**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS, Networking, and Compute Services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Advanced Networking, Hybrid Storage, and Backup Strategies](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Advanced Storage, Data Migration, and Cost Optimization](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Security Core, Database Services, and Encryption](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Containerization, Data Lake, and NoSQL Deep Dive](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Nginx, Serverless, Microservices, and CI/CD Automation](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [AWS Cloud Practitioner Study and Core Services Review](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Exam Preparation, Advanced Services, and Project Kickoff](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Project Init: Next.js, Design System, and Authentication](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Core Features: Wallet, Jars System, and Budget Logic](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [AI Integration, Chatbot, and Advanced Analytics](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Production Deployment (AWS/Docker) and Final Polish](1.12-week12/)
